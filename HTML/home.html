Promoting a Transformational Educational Experience

It is the responsibility of DAESA to cultivate a transformational educational experience for our students at WSU by providing pathways to exceptional resources and experiences that promote academic engagement and student achievement for all undergraduates. We accomplish this task through the combined efforts of over 20 units, programs, and sub-programs that make up our division in the Office of the Provost.
