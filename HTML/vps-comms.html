Vice Provost’s Communications

Stay up to date on official communications, policy statements, and guidance pertaining to undergraduate education from the Vice Provost for Academic Engagement and Student Achievement Mary Wack.

The University-wide programs under her direction support academic excellence for all students as well as outstanding teaching and learning in a land-grant, very high research university setting. In addition to these programs, she is also responsible for:

  • Providing guidance useful to WSU faculty on matters pertaining to undergraduate education in the form of policy documents.
  • Providing support to faculty that promotes their pursuit of excellence in teaching and learning practices.
  • Leading university initiatives that focus on assessing WSU’s undergraduate education activities in pursuit of its land-grant mission and maintaining university accreditation.
